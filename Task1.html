My Favourite Places
        Inside
        IIT Kharagpur Campus

There are many beautiful and fascinating spots on the IIT Kharagpur campus, but here are the top three that I always look forward to visiting:
  1. BR Ambedkar Hall Of Residence
  2. Main Academic Building
  3. Nehru Museum of Science and Technology

  • BR Ambedkar Hall Of Residence:'Home away from Home';This was the first place I went when I got to Kgp Campus. I can get away from the outside world and think quietly here. And the best thing about BRH is its delicious food.
  • IIT Kharagpur Main Building:When someone thinks of IIT-KGP, this is the image that comes to mind, and it is the dream of lakhs of JEE aspirants to get here. I remember posing for the photographs alongside my parents at this picturesque spot just before the induction began.
  • Nehru Museum:The significance of this location in history is immense. Freedom fighters were once imprisoned here. Many people visit the museum every day because of the stunning architecture and tranquilly surrounding it.
